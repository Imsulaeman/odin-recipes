Steamed Chicken

The comfort foods you only need. the mom's cooking that you will always miss.

Ingridients

  • 4 slices boneless chicken breast
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon pepper
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 550 ML Water

Steps

  1. Place your chicken in a large bowl and add the salt, oil and pepper. Give the chicken a good coating. Once done, add the fillets to the deep steaming tray.
  2. Add your water to the mixing bowl and insert the deep steaming set. Steam your chicken using the steaming mode for 15 minutes.
  3. Enjoy your dish!
